CHRISTMAS LISTS

Evan Keeton

Why GitHub & HashBase.IO?

I chose to create my Christmas Lists on GitHub, hosted through HashBase.IO, because HashBase provides an easy way to host HTML with a link for anyone to view. I needed HTML because, nerd that I am, my Christmas lists have become too complex for any other format.

Why HTML?

"So why HTML?" you ask. Well, as stated above, nerds like me often refuse to do things the easy way. As I've become more of a nerd, I've found myself more and more interested in complex structures—for better or for worse—and Christmas has not escaped my wrath. I’ve recently begun to envision "gift packages" of more than one gift, and to easily distribute these ideas to my relatives, I need HTML to group the necessary links together. Also, to a nerd, this way is just more fun.
